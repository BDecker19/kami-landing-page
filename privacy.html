Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 7, 2026

Kami ("Kami," "we," "our," or "us") provides a location-aware social discovery application that helps users discover and connect with other users nearby.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

By using Kami, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create or use a Kami account, we may collect:

  • Name or display name
  • Username
  • Profile photo
  • Bio or profile information
  • Instagram account information you choose to connect
  • Communications you send to us

Instagram Authentication & Connected Content

If you choose to connect Instagram through Meta’s official authentication flow, we may import certain profile information you authorize us to access, such as:

  • Username
  • Profile picture
  • Bio
  • Basic account information permitted by Meta’s APIs
  • Content or media you explicitly authorize us to access through Meta’s APIs

We may analyze authorized profile information and connected content in order to:

  • Personalize user profiles
  • Improve recommendations and nearby discovery
  • Generate profile summaries, interests, or relevance signals
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

We only access information you explicitly authorize through the official Meta authentication process.

Kami does not scrape Instagram profiles or access private Instagram content without authorization.

Location Information

Kami is a location-aware application and may collect:

  • Precise location data
  • Background location data
  • Approximate location data
  • Venue or proximity-related signals

We use this information to power nearby discovery, relevance, notifications, and app functionality.

You can control location permissions through your device settings.

Device & Usage Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • IP address
  • Session activity
  • Crash reports
  • Analytics and usage data

2. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve Kami services
  • Create and maintain user profiles
  • Help users discover relevant nearby users
  • Personalize app experiences
  • Improve recommendations and notifications
  • Authenticate users
  • Analyze connected content to improve personalization and relevance
  • Prevent spam, abuse, or fraudulent activity
  • Monitor app performance and reliability
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Location Services

Kami uses location information to enable core app functionality, including nearby social discovery and context-aware experiences.

Depending on your device permissions, Kami may collect location information while the app is open or running in the background.

You can disable location access at any time through your device settings, though some features may stop functioning properly.

4. How Information Is Shared

We may share limited information:

  • With other Kami users as part of the app experience
  • With service providers that help us operate the app
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the safety, rights, or integrity of Kami and its users
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to operate and improve Kami, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Users may request account deletion at any time.

6. Your Choices

You may:

  • Edit your profile information
  • Disconnect Instagram access
  • Disable location permissions
  • Request account deletion
  • Stop using Kami at any time

To request deletion of your account or data, contact: privacy@kamisocial.com

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect user information.

However, no system can be guaranteed completely secure.

8. Age Requirements

Kami is intended for users 16 years of age or older. Users under 16 may not use the Services.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

9. International Users

Kami may process and store information in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

By using Kami, you consent to such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make material changes, we may notify users through the app or by other means.

Continued use of Kami after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
