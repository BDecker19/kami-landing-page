Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We may collect profile information, Instagram account information you choose to connect, location data, device information, and analytics data.

If you choose to connect Instagram through Meta's official authentication flow, we may import profile information and analyze authorized content to personalize recommendations and improve nearby discovery.

Kami does not scrape Instagram profiles or access private Instagram content without authorization.

Location Services

Kami uses location information to power nearby discovery and context-aware experiences. You can control location permissions through your device settings.
